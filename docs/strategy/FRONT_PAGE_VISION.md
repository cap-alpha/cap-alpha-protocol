# Front Page Vision & Identity

## The Core Product Positioning: "The Bloomberg Terminal for NFL Capital"
Cap Alpha Protocol is not a standard sports statistics website—it is an **Intelligence Aggregator and Prediction Market** designed to evaluate the $200B NFL human capital market. 

The Front Page must immediately communicate premium, high-stakes financial analysis rather than traditional "Fan UI." The aesthetic should evoke high-frequency trading terminals, institutional dashboards, and live market moving data.

## Persona Integration Strategy
The Front Page must serve as the universal funnel for our four distinct users without overwhelming any single one.

### 1. The Shark (Agent Persona)
- **Primary Need:** Leverage. Proof of asset mispricing.
- **Front Page Component:** **The Asset Ledger.** 
  - A real-time ticker or ranking board displaying the most heavily *Undervalued* assets (players producing surplus value against their cap hit). This immediately provides agents with visual, shareable proof of leverage for their clients.

### 2. The Sharp (Bettor Persona)
- **Primary Need:** Real-time information asymmetry. Spread impact.
- **Front Page Component:** **The Live Intelligence Feed.**
  - A low-latency streaming sidebar or marquee displaying real-time injury reports, contract restructures, and media sentiment shifts. If a star player's sentiment plunges, the Sharp needs to see it *before* the Vegas line moves.

### 3. The Armchair GM (Fan Persona)
- **Primary Need:** Social engagement, tribal dunking, shareability.
- **Front Page Component:** **The Tactical Trade Engine Call-to-Action.**
  - A massive, central, highly visual search/select box urging the user to "Propose a Blockbuster." The UI must focus on the generation of an immediate, simple "A-to-F" algorithmic grade that is perfectly formatted for a one-click Twitter/X export.

### 4. The Suit (Front Office Persona)
- **Primary Need:** Discretion, risk mitigation, ledger accuracy.
- **Front Page Component:** **The Market Efficiency Index.**
  - A macro-level chart showing positional spending trends across the league vs. Super Bowl contention. This proves the system's validity and institutional capability before the Suit ever logs in to analyze their private roster data.

## Structural Layout (Low-Fidelity Concept)
**Hero Section:** 
- Left: Dynamic *Market Efficiency Index* (League-wide macro view).
- Right: *The Tactical Trade Engine* (Interactive CTA for Armchair GMs).

**Below The Fold:**
- Three-column layout:
  1. *The Long List*: Top 10 Undervalued Assets (For Agents).
  2. *The Short List*: Top 10 Overvalued Assets / Dead Money Risks (For Front Offices).
  3. *The Wire*: Streaming Intelligence Feed (For Bettors).

## Non-Negotiable Aesthetics (Tufte/UX Rules)
- **No Chartjunk:** Data density must remain high. No gratuitous logos where team colors or abbreviations suffice.
- **Dark Mode Native:** Deep zincs, emeralds (surplus), and rose (dead money) to enforce the financial/quant application identity.
- **Zero-State Grace:** If MotherDuck connection is hydrating, display elegant skeletal loaders, never generic spinners or "No Data" walls.
